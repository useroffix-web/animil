  - her-eyes.jpg      → Used in the "Look Into Her Eyes" heartbeat game
  - us-1.jpg          → "You & Me" gallery — photo 1
  - us-2.jpg          → "You & Me" gallery — photo 2
  - us-3.jpg          → "You & Me" gallery — photo 3 (featured/wide)
  - us-4.jpg          → "You & Me" gallery — photo 4
  - us-5.jpg          → "You & Me" gallery — photo 5

Then update index.html:
  1. Find the photo-placeholder div in the section you want to update
  2. Replace the placeholder with an <img> tag, e.g.:
     <img src="images/her-smile.jpg" alt="Her beautiful smile" />
  3. For the "You & Me" gallery, replace each gallery-placeholder div with:
     <img src="images/us-1.jpg" alt="Us together" />
     (and remove the gallery-placeholder div)
